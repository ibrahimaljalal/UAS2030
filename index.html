    About

    From KFUPM university UAS2030 team was founded by Dr. Ayman Abdallah(1) in 2019. The team has participated three times in SUAS (2) competition. In their first attempt in 2022 they scored the 11the rank out of 71 universities. In their third attempt in 2024 they scored the first rank out of 70 universities and won the best mission award.

    1. Dr. Ayman Abdallah is the Chairman of Aerospace Engineering Department & the director of IRC-ASE (1)

      1. Interdisciplinary Research Center for Aviation & Space Exploration

    2. SUAS is a yearly global competition in the United Stats of America. The competition has been going on for more than 20 years
